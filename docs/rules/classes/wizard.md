# Class: Wizard

| Property | Value |
|---|---|

| Armor    | None; no shields |
| Weapons  | Dagger or staff only |
| Key Stat | INT |

## Class Features

### Spellcasting
Cast spells using **INT modifier**. See spellcasting.md for the full casting rules.
On a **natural 1**, the spell fails, is lost until rest, and you roll on the
**Mishap Table** for that spell's tier — a table of harmful or chaotic magical effects.

### Learning Spells from Scrolls
Study a scroll for **one full day**, then make a **DC 15 INT check**.
- **Success:** The spell is permanently added to your known spells.
- **Failure:** You can try again after another day of study.

### Bonus Languages
Wizards start knowing **2 common languages and 2 rare languages** of their choice.

## Talent Table

Roll 2d6 at levels 1, 3, 5, 7, and 9. Humans roll twice at level 1.

| 2d6 | Result |
|---|---|
| 2     | Gain a new Wizard spell of any tier you can cast |
| 3–6   | +1 to spellcasting checks |
| 7–9   | +2 to INT, WIS, or CHA (your choice) |
| 10–11 | +1 to spellcasting checks; or gain a new Tier 1 spell |
| 12    | Choose any Wizard Talent or gain a new spell |
