# Class: Priest

## At a Glance

| Property | Value |
|---|---|
| Hit Die  | d6 per level |
| Armor    | Any armor; shields |
| Weapons  | Piercing and slashing melee weapons; limited ranged |
| Key Stat | WIS |

## Class Features

### Spellcasting
Cast spells using **WIS modifier**. See spellcasting.md for the full casting rules.
On a **natural 1**, the spell fails, is lost until rest, and your deity revokes access —
ritual penance is required before that spell returns.

### Turn Undead
A free ability — does not count against spells known.
Make a **spellcasting roll** (1d20 + WIS modifier, DC varies by undead level).
On success, undead of equal or lower level **flee or are paralyzed**.

### Deity and Alignment
You must choose a deity whose alignment matches your own.
You gain one bonus language based on your deity's domain:
- Celestial (holy deities)
- Diabolic (dark deities)
- Primordial (nature/primal deities)

## Talent Table

Roll 2d6 at levels 1, 3, 5, 7, and 9. Humans roll twice at level 1.

| 2d6 | Result |
|---|---|
| 2     | Gain a new Priest spell of any tier you can cast |
| 3–6   | +1 to spellcasting checks |
| 7–9   | +2 to WIS, STR, or CHA (your choice) |
| 10–11 | +1 to spellcasting checks and Turn Undead rolls |
| 12    | Choose any Priest Talent or gain a new spell |
