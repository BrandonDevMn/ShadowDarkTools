# Equipment

## Gear Slots (Carry Limit)

A character can carry gear slots equal to their **STR score or 10, whichever is higher**.

| STR Score | Gear Slots |
|---|---|
| 1–10 | 10 slots (floor) |
| 11   | 11 slots |
| 12   | 12 slots |
| … | … |
| 18   | 18 slots |

**Exceptions:**
- The first **backpack** does not count against your slots
- The first **100 coins** do not count against your slots
- **Fighter Hauler** feature: add your positive CON modifier to total slots

## Starting Gold

Roll **2d6 × 5** gold pieces at 1st level.

## Armor

| Armor      | Cost   | Slots | AC |
|---|---|---|---|
| Leather    | 10 gp  | 1 | 11 + DEX mod |
| Chainmail  | 60 gp  | 2 | 13 + DEX mod (disadvantage on stealth and swimming) |
| Plate Mail | 130 gp | 3 | 15 (no swimming; disadvantage on stealth) |
| Shield     | 10 gp  | 1 | +2 AC; occupies one hand |

**Mithral upgrade** (applied to any metal armor): ×4 base cost, −1 slot, removes all stealth and swim penalties.
