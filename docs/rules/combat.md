# Combat

## Initiative

Every participant (players and the GM for each monster group) rolls **1d20 + DEX modifier**.
Highest result acts first. Ties are broken by the players (players win ties vs. monsters).
Turn order then proceeds clockwise around the table for the rest of the combat.

## On Your Turn

- Take **one action** (attack, cast a spell, use an item, attempt a check, etc.)
- Move up to **Near** distance (≈30 feet)
- If you forgo your action entirely, you may move Near a second time

## Distances

Shadowdark uses abstract range bands instead of precise grids:

| Band  | Approx. Distance | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Close | ≈5 feet   | Adjacent; required for most melee weapons |
| Near  | ≈30 feet  | Standard move distance; most spells reach here |
| Far   | Line of sight | Ranged weapons reach here |

## Armor Class

- **Unarmored:** AC = 10 + DEX modifier
- **Armor:** See equipment.md for AC values by armor type
- **Shield:** +2 AC; occupies one hand

## Attack Rolls

- **Melee:** 1d20 + STR modifier (+ Talent bonuses) vs. target AC
- **Ranged:** 1d20 + DEX modifier (+ Talent bonuses) vs. target AC
- Meet or beat the target's AC to hit

## Damage

Roll the weapon's damage die. **No ability modifier is added to damage.**
Damage bonuses come only from Talents or magic items.

## Critical Hit (Natural 20)

Double the weapon's damage dice — roll them twice and add both results.

## Natural 1 on Attack

Automatic miss. The GM may rule that you strike an ally or drop your weapon.

## Morale

When enemies are reduced to **half their starting number** (or a solo enemy drops to **half HP**),
they must make a **DC 15 WIS check** or flee immediately.
