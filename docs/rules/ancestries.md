# Ancestries

Each character chooses one ancestry at creation. Ancestries replace the word "race."
Every ancestry grants exactly one mechanical trait.

## Core Ancestries

| Ancestry | Trait |
|---|---|
| **Dwarf**    | +2 HP at 1st level; roll hit points each level with advantage |
| **Elf**      | +1 to ranged attack rolls **or** +1 to spellcasting checks (choose one at character creation) |
| **Goblin**   | Cannot be surprised |
| **Half-Orc** | +1 to attack rolls **and** damage rolls with melee weapons |
| **Halfling** | Once per day, become invisible for 3 rounds |
| **Human**    | Gain one additional Talent roll at 1st level |

## Starting Languages

| Ancestry | Languages Known |
|---|---|
| Human    | Common + one additional language of your choice |
| Elf      | Three languages |
| Most others | Two languages |
